The Table

A place set with intention. Where everyday moments become something worth lingering in.

The table is where everything gathers — the food, the light, the conversation, the quiet in-between moments. It doesn't need to be perfect to feel complete. A few thoughtful pieces, a sense of balance, and a willingness to slow down are enough. Some pieces are found, some are kept, and a few are linked here if you're looking for something similar.

What's here are the pieces I reach for again and again — not because they're special on their own, but because of how they come together.
